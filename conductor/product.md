# Product Guide

## Initial Concept
An enterprise-grade, offline-capable identity resolution and training governance portal for Mahindra & Mahindra Tractors.

## Target Audience
- **HR & Training Managers:** Personnel managing the master roster and training records.
- **Regional Supervisors:** Managers tracking local manpower and training KPIs across plants.

## Primary Business Value
- **Identity Resolution:** The core focus is on resolving identities across disparate data sources, aligning variable column headers, and performing 7-pass deduplication to maintain a pristine master roster.

## Success Metrics
- **Processing Efficiency:** The primary metric for success is minimizing manual audit exceptions and drastically reducing data processing turnaround times.

## Deployment & Expansion Strategy
- **Standalone Web Portal:** The platform will operate as an isolated, standalone web-based portal, completely offline and disconnected from external enterprise networks.
