# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
- **Professional & Direct:** Use concise, formal, and objective language across all interface labels, instructions, and generated reports. Avoid conversational filler or overly technical jargon where possible.

## Branding and Visual Design
- **Corporate Industrial:** The visual style should be clean, functional, and high-contrast, reflecting the robust and reliable nature of the Mahindra & Mahindra industrial brand. Focus on clarity and structural integrity in the layout.

## User Experience (UX) Principles
- **Interactive Exploration:** The dashboard must enable users to perform deep-dives into data through complex filtering, interactive charts, and drill-down capabilities.
- **Guidance-Heavy:** Provide clear, explicit instructions and contextual help (tooltips, info boxes) at every step of the workflow to minimize user error and onboarding friction.

## Component Library and Design System
- **Streamlit-Native:** Adhere strictly to Streamlit's native layout patterns and component behaviors. Utilize the standard sidebar for navigation and primary controls to ensure a consistent and predictable user experience.
